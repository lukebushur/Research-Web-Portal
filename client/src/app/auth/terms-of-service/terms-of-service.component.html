GPA Disclosure and FERPA Compliance

As part of your profile on this portal, you may choose to voluntarily provide your Grade Point Average (GPA). By entering this information, you are giving written consent, in accordance with the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA), for this academic information to be shared with faculty users on the platform under the following conditions:

  • Your GPA will only be visible to faculty users whose projects you actively apply to.

  • Your GPA will not be visible to other students or to faculty members not associated with your applications.

  • Providing your GPA is completely optional. However, some projects may require GPA information for application eligibility or review.

  • You may modify or remove your GPA at any time in your account settings.

By submitting your GPA, you acknowledge that you are voluntarily disclosing this information and consent to its limited use as described above.
